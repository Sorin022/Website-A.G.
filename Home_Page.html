An experienced Clinical Psychologist


Welcome! I am Dr. Audrey Gelfand. I am a clinical Psychologist with a Ph.D. from Hofstra University and a master’s degree in School Psychology. I have been in private practice since 2000. Before that, I worked as a school psychologist and in a day treatment program for young children. In my clinical practice I work with children, adolescents, and adults. My training is within the cognitive behavioral model of treatment; however, I bring many other things to treatment and develop an individual style based on what I determine a client needs. My work isn’t cookie cutter or developed from a workbook. Therapy with me is based upon 25 years of experience and a skill in putting together various styles and interventions from different perspectives. I believe in each person’s individuality and in identifying strengths and talents so we can build upon those. I also believe in utilizing a person’s natural support system (friends and family) whenever possible. I work extensively with local Psychiatrists when medication is a part of treatment and can also work with school psychologists or social workers when needed. Compassion and understanding are important to my work and I think it’s critical to develop a trusting relationship with my patients. This trust allows us to work together to bring about change. Therapy is very challenging and can feel daunting to new patients, however, in a supportive environment, it is extremely helpful. I am very grateful to those people who put their trust in me.
